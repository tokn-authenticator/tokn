Tokn is a minimal 2FA / MFA TOTP/HOTP authenticator that keeps your one-time codes encrypted on-device. No accounts, no cloud, no trackers.

Features:

* Standard otpauth:// URIs (TOTP, HOTP, SHA-1 / SHA-256 / SHA-512)
* Encrypted local vault
* Unlock with biometrics (with password fallback) or password only
* Add accounts by scanning a QR code with the camera or from a gallery image
* Encrypted backup and restore
* Direct device-to-device sync over Wi-Fi Direct, Local Wi-Fi or QR Code. Nothing leaves your local network, the handshake is end-to-end encrypted
* Material 3 UI
* No tracking, no ads, no Google Play Services required

You want to move from a other 2FA App? No problem: Tokn supports importing backups from Aegis, 2FAS and Google Authenticator (more soon)
